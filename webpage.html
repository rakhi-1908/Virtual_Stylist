To determine your body type, you can follow this simple guide:

Step 1: Take Key Measurements Use a flexible measuring tape and follow these steps: 1. Shoulders: - Measure the widest part of your shoulders by wrapping the tape all the way around. This is easier with assistance. 2. Bust: - Measure around the fullest part of your bust, ensuring the tape is level across your back and chest. 3. Waist: - Find the narrowest part of your waist, typically just above your belly button, and measure around it. 4. Hips: - Measure around the fullest part of your hips and buttocks.
